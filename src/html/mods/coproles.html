These are some alternative careers for law enforcement based on the NPC stats from Protect & Serve. NOTE: As a homebrew ruling, the Authority special ability encompasses the skill Expert(Law) and isn't required to take in addition.

Law Enforcement Admin

A law enforcement administration is trained as an officer, but decided or was force to be a desk worker. They are not out fighting crime, but processing the paperwork and doing other task to support a department. They have been known to be called on to add to the ranks when riots are too much and more bodies are needed.
Skills
  • Authority
  • Accounting
  • Awareness/Notice
  • Bureaucracy
  • Education
  • Forgery
  • Handgun
  • Human Perception
  • Interview
  • Martial Arts (choose one: Aikido, Judo, Sambo, Wrestling)

Forensic Technician

Forensic technician work along side the Investigation division of the law enforcement. There are specialists within this role that can concentrate on things like business fraud, ballistic analysis, blood splatter, and other sciences.
Skills
  • Authority
  • Awareness/Notice
  • Basic Tech
  • Chemistry
  • Education
  • Handgun
  • Library Search
  • Martial Arts (choose one: Aikido, Judo, Sambo, Wrestling)
  • Photo/Film
  • Choose One: Accounting, Biology, Expert (choose), Forgery, Geology, Physics, System Knowledge
