Cybersmily's Datafort

Fansite for R. Talsorian's Cyberpunk 2020

Welcome to Cybersmily's Datafort!

This site uses HTML5 and isn't supported in older browsers! Please change to a modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.

This site was created back in the late 90's as an unofficial fan site dedicated to the R. Talsorian's tabletop RPG Cyberpunk 2020 and Cyberpunk Red as well as Monster Fight Club Cyberpunk Red: Combat Zone. Here you will find articles, web page apps, and other unofficial resources for CyberPunk 2020, Cyberpunk Red, & Cyberpunk Red: Combat Zone.
